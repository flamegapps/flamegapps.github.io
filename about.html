About the Project

FlameGApps are Google Apps packages for your Android devices We provide clean and optimized resource friendly Google Apps packages. We provide two editions.

- Basic edition which includes the core GApps like Google Play Services (GMS), Google Services Framework (GSF), Google Play Store and other essentials.

- Full edition, which includes all the necessary core apps with more useful Google apps like Google Dialer, Google Contacts, Google Messages, etc. We provide regular updates for all packages.
